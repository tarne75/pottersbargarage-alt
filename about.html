About Potters Bar Garage

A family-run business committed to honest, high-quality car care since 2009.

Our Story

We Care About More Than Your Car

Potters Bar Garage offers a comprehensive range of services for all types of cars and motorbikes. Our aim is to provide high-quality service at affordable prices. We welcome both private customers and fleet work, and are able to provide same-day servicing on all makes of private and light commercial vehicles for your convenience.

We provide comprehensive repair services under one roof — whether you need tyre replacement, MOT testing, servicing, diagnostics, air-conditioning, bodywork or spare parts, our team of experts are here to offer fair and honest advice.

Our expert technicians provide all the major system checks and adjustments for all makes and models. We take pride in every job, regardless of size, and only use reputable brands that produce excellent quality parts to maximise the reliability and performance of your vehicle.

All our work is fully guaranteed for one year. If anything goes wrong with what we've fitted or the work we've completed, we take full responsibility.

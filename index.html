About Us

The Name You Can Trust

Potters Bar Garage is a family-run business based in Potters Bar, Hertfordshire. We provide car and motorbike repair services for all makes and models, with specialist experience in Jaguar, Land Rover, Citroën and Ford.

Our 3-bay workshop includes a full MOT testing facility for Class I & II motorcycles and Class IV cars, minibuses and vans up to 3,000 kg. We're also proud to operate a HUNTER PRO ALIGN 4-wheel alignment machine and a state-of-the-art off-site bodyshop.

Fully Guaranteed

All our work is guaranteed for 12 months — no small print.

Latest Equipment

Modern diagnostics and specialist tools for all vehicles.

Expert Technicians

Fully trained staff with decades of hands-on experience.

Reputable Parts

Only quality branded parts used — maximising reliability.

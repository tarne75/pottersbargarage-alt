Our Services

Comprehensive auto care for cars and motorbikes — all makes, all models, all under one roof.

What We Offer

Everything Your Vehicle Needs

From routine maintenance to specialist repairs — Potters Bar Garage has it covered. All work is fully guaranteed for 12 months.

Servicing

We provide full and interim servicing for all makes and models of cars and light commercial vehicles. Our same-day service covers oil changes, filter replacements, fluid top-ups, brake inspections and a comprehensive multi-point vehicle check — keeping your vehicle running safely and efficiently.

MOT Testing

Our 3-bay workshop includes a full MOT testing facility for Class I & II motorcycles and Class IV cars, minibuses and vans up to 3,000 kg. Our experienced mechanics carry out thorough tests at competitive prices, with prompt re-testing if required. Car MOT from £130, bike MOT from £100.

Diagnostics

We use approved, up-to-date diagnostic tools to find faults quickly across all makes and models. Whether it's a warning light, misfiring engine or unexplained performance issue, we'll identify the root cause accurately — saving you time and money on unnecessary guesswork.

Brakes

We inspect brake pads, shoes, discs and callipers on all vehicle types, and advise you honestly on what's unsafe and what can safely wait. Brake safety is critical — we never recommend unnecessary work, but we'll always flag a genuine risk. All brake parts carry our 12-month guarantee.

Batteries

We test your battery's health and recommend the best replacement option if necessary. We stock a wide range of batteries for all vehicle makes and models, and can usually fit your new battery on the spot while you wait. Cold-weather checks also available.

Air Conditioning

Whether you need a re-gas, a full air-con service or a refrigerant leak investigation, we make it a breeze. A properly functioning air-con system improves driving comfort and cabin visibility in all seasons. Quick turnaround with same-day availability on most jobs.

Exhausts

We provide comprehensive car exhaust repairs and full replacement services. From silencer fixes to complete system replacements, we carry a wide range of exhaust components for all popular vehicle makes and models, fitted quickly with quality-guaranteed parts.

Bodywork

Potters Bar Garage operates a state-of-the-art bodyshop and mixing room at our modern off-site facility. We handle everything from minor dent removal and scratch repair to full resprays — all colour-matched precisely to your vehicle's original finish. Independent body repair and wheel refurbishment also available.

Wheel Alignment

We're proud to operate a HUNTER PRO ALIGN 4-wheel laser alignment machine — one of the most accurate systems available. Correct alignment improves tyre longevity, fuel economy and handling safety. If your car pulls to one side or you've hit a kerb recently, book in for a check.

Electric & Hybrid

We provide professional servicing and repairs for electric and hybrid vehicles of all makes, including battery health checks, regenerative braking inspections, software diagnostics and routine maintenance. As EV ownership grows, we're ready to keep your green vehicle in peak condition.
